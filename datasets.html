Datasets

  • Dataset 1: Mario PCG

    Dataset of seventy-four humans playing through eleven levels from three AI generators and one true Super Mario Bros. level. It includes images of the levels, java-representations of the levels, logs of player actions, and ranking results from a player survey. If you make use of this dataset please cite "Game Level Generation from Gameplay Videos" by Matthew Guzdial and Mark O Riedl from the Twelfth Artificial Intelligence and Interactive Digital Entertainment Conference.

    Download the data here

  • Dataset 2: "Gwario"

    Dataset of sixty-four humans playing through four levels of levels adapted from Super Mario Bros.: The Lost Levels for a human computation tasks. It includes c# representations of the levels, logs of player actions, and ranking results from a player survey. Notably the dataset includes both singleplayer and multiplayer results. If you make use of this dataset please cite "Evaluating Singleplayer and Multiplayer in Human Computation Games" by Kristin Siu, Matthew Guzdial, and Mark O Riedl from the Foundations of Digitial Games Conference 2017.
